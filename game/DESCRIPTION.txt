The QBert game provides a pink cube character for the player and has 3 enemies.

The player loses the game if:
	1. He is on the same cube as any of the enemies
	2. He jumps off the board

The player wins if:
	1. He succesfully traverses all the cubes without losing. The color of the traversed cubes is changed to make it easy for the player.

The spinning disc takes the player to the top of the board.

Technologies used: HTML-5, JavaScript, WebGL.
